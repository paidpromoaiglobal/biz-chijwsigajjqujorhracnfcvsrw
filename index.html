PetPlus Pet Clinic – Waggy Tails is a premier veterinary care center located on OMR Road, Kelambakkam, Chennai, dedicated to providing compassionate and professional healthcare for your beloved pets. Open 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, our experienced team of veterinarians and groomers ensures your furry family members receive the attention and treatment they deserve at any hour. With a stellar 4.1-star rating and hundreds of happy pet parents, we are proud to be one of the most trusted pet clinics in the area.
